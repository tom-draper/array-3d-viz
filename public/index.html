Why Use Array Visualizer?

Instant Visualization

Paste your data and see it rendered in 3D immediately. No setup, no installation required.

100% Private

Interactive Controls

Rotate, zoom, and explore your data from every angle with smooth 3D navigation.

Data Distribution

View statistical distributions and query specific values or ranges in real-time.

Fast & Responsive

Completely Free

Open source and free to use for personal, educational, and commercial projects.

How It Works

1

Input Your Data

Paste your JSON or CSV array into the text field above. The tool automatically detects the format and structure.

2

Click Visualize

Hit the "Visualize in 3D" button and watch your data transform into an interactive 3D scene.

3

Explore & Analyze

Use mouse controls to rotate, zoom, and pan. Query specific values and view distribution statistics.
