Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 2025-07-07

This Privacy Policy describes how this website ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and shares information when you use our JSON data visualization services.

Quick Summary: We don't collect or store your personal data or JSON files. However, we use Google AdSense for advertising, which may collect information about you through cookies and similar technologies.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

When you use our JSON visualization tool, the data you input is processed entirely in your browser and is not transmitted to or stored on our servers. We do not have access to, collect, or retain any JSON data you visualize.

Information Collected by Third Parties

We use Google AdSense to display advertisements on our website. Google AdSense may collect information about you, including:

  • Your IP address
  • Browser type and version
  • Operating system
  • Pages visited and time spent on our site
  • Referring website
  • General location information (city/region level)

Cookies and Similar Technologies

Our website itself does not use cookies. However, Google AdSense uses cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Serve personalized advertisements based on your interests
  • Measure ad performance and effectiveness
  • Remember your ad preferences
  • Prevent the same ads from being shown to you repeatedly

Google's advertising cookies enable Google and its partners to serve ads based on your visits to this website and other websites across the Internet.

How We Use Information

We use our website to provide JSON data visualization services. Since we don't collect personal information directly, we don't use your information for any purpose other than providing our visualization tool.

Google AdSense uses the information it collects to:

  • Display relevant advertisements
  • Measure and improve ad performance
  • Provide reporting to advertisers
  • Detect and prevent fraud

Your Choices and Rights

Opting Out of Personalized Advertising

You can opt out of personalized advertising by Google through the following methods:

  • Visit Google Ads Settings
  • Visit About Ads Choice
  • Use your browser's "Do Not Track" setting
  • Install browser extensions that block tracking

Managing Cookies

You can control cookies through your browser settings. However, disabling cookies may affect the functionality of advertisements on our site.

Data Retention

We do not retain any personal data as we do not collect it directly. Google AdSense retains data according to their own retention policies, which you can review in Google's Privacy Policy.

International Users

Our website is hosted and operated in Washington, USA. If you access our site from outside this region, please be aware that your information may be processed in countries with different privacy laws.

Security

Since we don't collect or store personal data, there are no security risks related to data breaches from our end. However, we recommend that you do not input sensitive or confidential information into any web-based tools.

Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to other websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of other websites. We encourage you to read the privacy policies of any linked websites.
